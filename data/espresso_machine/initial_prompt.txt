The Breville Barista Express Espresso Machine, model BES870XL, is a striking piece of kitchen equipment, crafted predominantly from brushed stainless steel, which gives it a sleek and modern appearance. The machine's robust build is evident in its substantial weight and the solid feel of its components, such as the heavy-duty portafilter and the integrated grinder. The stainless steel finish is not only visually appealing but also suggests durability, although some users have noted that it can be prone to rust if not properly maintained.

The machine's design is compact, making it suitable for most kitchen countertops, though its height may require consideration if placed under low-hanging cabinets. The front of the machine features a clear and functional layout, with a pressure gauge prominently displayed, providing instant feedback during the brewing process. This gauge, along with various knobs and buttons, allows for precise control over the espresso-making process, adding to the machine's professional aesthetic.

The integrated grinder is seamlessly incorporated into the machine's design, eliminating the need for a separate appliance and contributing to the Barista Express's streamlined look. The grinder's hopper is transparent, allowing users to easily monitor bean levels, and it is topped with a sleek, black lid that complements the stainless steel body.

The steam wand, also made of stainless steel, is designed for easy maneuverability and efficient milk frothing, capable of producing the microfoam necessary for latte art. The machine includes a stainless steel milk jug, which matches the overall aesthetic and adds to the cohesive design.

Overall, the Breville Barista Express is a visually impressive machine that combines functionality with a polished, professional look, making it a standout addition to any kitchen.
