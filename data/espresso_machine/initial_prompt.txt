The Breville Barista Express Espresso Machine, model BES870XL, is a striking piece of kitchen equipment, crafted predominantly from brushed stainless steel. This material gives the machine a sleek, modern appearance, with a subtle sheen that catches the light without being overly reflective. The stainless steel finish is complemented by a few plastic components, strategically placed to maintain durability while keeping the machine's weight manageable. The machine's design is compact, yet it exudes a robust, professional aura, reminiscent of high-end café equipment.

The front of the machine is dominated by a clear, easy-to-read pressure gauge, which is encased in a polished metal bezel, adding a touch of sophistication. Below the gauge, the control panel features a series of buttons and knobs, each with a tactile, satisfying click, allowing for precise adjustments. The integrated grinder is seamlessly incorporated into the left side of the machine, with a transparent bean hopper that allows you to see the beans inside, adding a functional yet decorative element.

The portafilter is a heavy-duty piece, made from stainless steel, ensuring it feels substantial and well-balanced in the hand. The steam wand, also stainless steel, is designed for easy maneuverability, allowing for precise frothing and steaming of milk. It is complemented by a stainless steel milk jug, which is included with the machine and matches its overall aesthetic.

The machine's overall build is sturdy, with a weight that suggests durability and quality. The brushed finish is resistant to fingerprints, maintaining its pristine look with minimal upkeep. Despite its robust construction, the machine's design is elegant, with clean lines and a minimalist approach that ensures it will fit seamlessly into any modern kitchen setting.
