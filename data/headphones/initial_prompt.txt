The Beats Studio3 Wireless Noise Cancelling Over-Ear Headphones in blue present a sleek and modern aesthetic, characterized by their bold, deep blue color that covers the entire exterior of the headphones. The finish is smooth and matte, giving them a sophisticated look that resists fingerprints and smudges. The ear cups are large and oval-shaped, designed to envelop the ears completely, providing a snug fit that aids in noise cancellation. The padding on the ear cups is plush and thick, covered in a soft, leather-like material that adds to the luxurious feel, though some users have noted that it can wear over time.

The headband is sturdy, with a metal core that provides durability, yet it is wrapped in the same blue material as the ear cups, maintaining a cohesive design. The hinges, which allow the headphones to fold for storage, are robust but have been described as feeling slightly fragile, suggesting a need for careful handling. The overall construction is lightweight, yet some users have mentioned a noticeable clamping force, which can be uncomfortable during extended use.

The headphones feature minimalistic branding, with the iconic Beats logo subtly embossed on the sides of the ear cups, adding a touch of elegance without being overly conspicuous. The controls are seamlessly integrated into the design, with buttons that blend into the surface of the ear cups, maintaining the clean lines and uninterrupted flow of the design. The headphones come with a matching hard shell case for protection, which is compact yet spacious enough to accommodate the folded headphones and accessories. Overall, the Beats Studio3 headphones are a visually striking piece of audio equipment, combining style with functionality in a way that appeals to both tech enthusiasts and fashion-conscious users.
